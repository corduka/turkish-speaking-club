# Perfect Menemen Recipe 🇹🇷

Menemen is a classic Turkish breakfast dish made with eggs, tomatoes, peppers, and olive oil. It is simple, fresh, and incredibly delicious when cooked correctly. The secret to a perfect menemen is using **ripe tomatoes**, **good olive oil**, and **not overcooking the eggs**.

---

## Ingredients (Serves 2)

- 3 large eggs
- 2 ripe tomatoes (or 1 cup grated tomatoes)
- 2 green peppers (Turkish sivri biber if possible)
- 2 tablespoons olive oil
- 1 small onion *(optional — traditional debate in Turkey)*
- 1 teaspoon salt
- 1/2 teaspoon black pepper
- 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (pul biber)
- Optional toppings:
  - White cheese (feta or beyaz peynir)
  - Parsley
  - Butter

---

## Equipment

- Medium frying pan
- Wooden spoon
- Knife
- Cutting board

---

## Preparation

1. **Chop the vegetables**
   - Finely chop the peppers.
   - Dice the tomatoes or grate them.
   - If using onion, chop it very finely.

2. **Heat the olive oil**
   - Place the pan on medium heat.
   - Add olive oil and let it warm for about 30 seconds.

3. **Cook the peppers**
   - Add the chopped peppers.
   - Cook for about **2–3 minutes** until they soften.

4. **Add the onions (optional)**
   - If using onion, add it now.
   - Cook until slightly soft and fragrant.

5. **Add the tomatoes**
   - Pour the tomatoes into the pan.
   - Add salt, black pepper, and red pepper flakes.
   - Cook for **5–7 minutes**, stirring occasionally, until the tomatoes thicken.

6. **Add the eggs**
   - Crack the eggs directly into the pan.
   - Gently mix them with the tomato mixture.

7. **Cook gently**
   - Stir slowly.
   - The eggs should stay **soft and slightly creamy**.

8. **Remove from heat**
   - Do not overcook. Menemen should be slightly juicy.

---

## Serving

Serve immediately while hot.

Traditionally served with:

- Fresh **Turkish bread**
- **Olives**
- **White cheese**
- **Tea (çay)**

Use bread to scoop the menemen directly from the pan.

---

## Pro Tips

- Use **very ripe tomatoes** for the best flavor.
- Cook on **medium heat** to avoid burning the tomatoes.
- Eggs should stay **soft**, not dry.
- Some people add **butter at the end** for extra richness.

---

## Variations

**Cheese Menemen**
Add crumbled feta or beyaz peynir when adding the eggs.

**Sucuklu Menemen**
Add slices of Turkish sausage (sucuk) before the tomatoes.

**Spicy Menemen**
Increase the amount of pul biber or add fresh chili.

---

## Quick Version (10 Minutes)

1. Heat olive oil.
2. Cook peppers.
3. Add tomatoes and spices.
4. Add eggs.
5. Stir gently.
6. Serve with bread.
